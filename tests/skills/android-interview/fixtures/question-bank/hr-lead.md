## Question

Tell me about a time you had to resolve delivery pressure or conflict across teams as a senior engineer or TL candidate.

## Intent

Evaluate communication maturity, prioritization, and influence under pressure.

## Follow-ups

- How did you frame the tradeoff to stakeholders?
- What did you learn from that situation?

## Scoring Notes

- 1: abstract leadership claims, no situation
- 3: clear situation and workable action
- 5: demonstrates influence, prioritization, and reflection

## Red Flags

- Blames others
- No concrete action

## Good Signals

- Uses structured communication
- Shows stakeholder alignment
- Explains lessons learned
