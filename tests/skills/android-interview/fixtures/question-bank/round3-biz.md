## Question

Tell me about a project where you had to change the technical plan because of business constraints or delivery pressure.

## Intent

Evaluate whether the candidate can balance engineering quality, business priority, and cross-team execution.

## Follow-ups

- What business constraint forced the change?
- How did you keep engineering risk under control?

## Scoring Notes

- 1: only talks about pressure, no decision logic
- 3: can explain the constraint and one practical adjustment
- 5: explains prioritization logic, technical compromise, and cross-team execution outcome

## Red Flags

- Treats business constraint as someone else's problem
- No ownership of the final execution plan

## Good Signals

- Explains tradeoffs clearly
- Connects engineering work to business outcomes
- Shows structured cross-team execution
