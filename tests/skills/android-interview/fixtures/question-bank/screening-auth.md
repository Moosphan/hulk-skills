## Question

Please walk me through the Android project you are most proud of and make it clear what you personally owned.

## Intent

Check whether the resume claims are backed by clear ownership and actual delivery responsibility.

## Follow-ups

- What part of the project was directly under your ownership?
- How did you measure the impact of your work?

## Scoring Notes

- 1: vague, no ownership boundary
- 3: clear project summary and partial ownership
- 5: explicit personal ownership, metrics, and tradeoffs

## Red Flags

- Overuses "we" without explaining personal contribution
- No measurable result

## Good Signals

- Clear ownership boundaries
- Concrete metrics
- Reasonable tradeoffs
