## Question

How do you handle team conflict when delivery pressure and quality expectations collide?

## Intent

Evaluate leadership maturity, conflict handling, and long-term collaboration style.

## Follow-ups

- How do you align different stakeholders?
- What makes you stay in or leave a team?

## Scoring Notes

- 1: vague conflict answer
- 3: can explain one real conflict and one alignment step
- 5: shows structured conflict handling, stakeholder alignment, and stable judgment

## Red Flags

- Blames others
- Cannot explain how they drove alignment

## Good Signals

- Explains the real tradeoff
- Uses calm stakeholder alignment
- Shows maturity about long-term fit
