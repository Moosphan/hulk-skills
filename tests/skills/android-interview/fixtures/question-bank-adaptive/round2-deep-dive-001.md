## Question

If you had to justify the startup strategy you chose over a more aggressive rewrite, how would you defend that tradeoff?

## Intent

Evaluate deep tradeoff reasoning, risk control, and senior-level judgment.

## Follow-ups

- What alternative did you reject and why?
- If you restarted today, what would you do differently?

## Scoring Notes

- 1: only repeats a decision
- 3: can compare two options and explain one risk
- 5: clearly defends the choice, rejected alternative, and rollout control

## Red Flags

- No alternative considered
- No explanation of the risk they accepted

## Good Signals

- Can explain why the slower path was safer
- Uses concrete rollout checkpoints
- Can revisit the tradeoff honestly
